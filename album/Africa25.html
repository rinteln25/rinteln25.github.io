South Africa

Worldwide known to be the fastest land mammal, cheetahs can reach up to 95 km/h in just 3 seconds. As they are smaller and more delicate than lions and leopards, they regularly loose prey to bigger predators. In combination with small genpool this is an explosive mixture for survival. Their population trend is decreasing and IUCN puts them in the category "vulnerable". Today, only around 6500 remain.
Maybe some of you think of them as ugly, but aren't they fascinating? The white-backed vultures are critically endangered due to trading and mass exctinction caused by us humans. Nevertheless, as scavengers they play a big part in the ecosystem. Therefore several stakeholders try to strenghten their protection with conservation actions.
Did you know that multiple small giraffs form a "Kindergarten"? Somethimes even hours on their own, their mothers are not far away.
Even though called on of Africas "ugliest 5", can you imagine something cuter than this hyena pub? With only a few month, they are incredibly curious and not shy at all!
African Wilddogs are one of the coolest animals in the african bush. Similar to wolves they hunt their prey to exhaustion, live in families and are led by a pair.
