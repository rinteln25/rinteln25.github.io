South Africa

The European Bee-eater went extinct in the 1980, but slowly started coming back to Germany. Although this return was not senn with delight, as people believed it made a deal with the devil because of its red eyes. Today you can find them all around the Kaiserstul in the loess soils.
This is one of the most colourful animals in Germany, the Western Green Lizzard. It only occurs in the south-west of Germany. The males impress with their blue head and throat (although the females are coloured blue as well even if not that strong). Are you impressed?
You've seen a bird hopping over the stones in the wild rushing river that looked similar to a blackbird female but has a white throat? Congratulations, you just discovered the white-throated dipper!
Beside some other butterflies, the European Peacock is one of the most beautiful ones found in Germany.
